Who We Are!

Welcome to "Now What? With Britt & Shawn!"

We're Britt and Shawn, a couple of educators here to help you find answers to the question "now what?" when it comes to learning. On our YouTube channel for grades K-6 we have fun learning new things, from math to art to science and more. Each week, we'll cook up a new hands-on activity for you to try. So grab a pencil, a paintbrush, a beaker or a spatula--things are about to get awesome!

Jumping Frog Craft.

Video of the Week!

In this episode, you'll learn how to make graffiti art. This activity is great for kids of all ages who can write their name. Whether you need a quick art lesson in the classroom or a fun activity at home, this lesson is simple and engaging. The lesson includes a background of what graffiti is and an easy step-by-step tutorial on how to make your own. All you need are three markers, a piece of paper, and lots of enthusiasm!

  • 1 Piece of White Paper.
  • 3 to 4 Coloured Markers.
  • A Flat Surface.
  • A Name.
  • Enthusiasm!
  • Imagination!

  1. Write Your Name.
  2. Thicken Your Letters.
  3. Add Shapes.
  4. Outline Your Letters and Shapes.
  5. Add Haze.
  6. Add Flare.

Interactive Physical Education Videos!

Welcome to Britt and Shawn Present Mission Possible. Join Britt and Shawn as they race across the globe, time, and sometimes the universe to save the day! Here are just a few of our super fun missions. Whether doing them by yourself or with others, in the classroom or at home, these missions are guaranteed to get your feet moving and heart racing. For access to more missions, you can visit our YouTube channel!

Welcome to Mission Possible: Moon Mission! In this fun and interactive physical education game, you will be tasked with collecting oxygen tanks, grabbing keys to the rocket ship, and finally getting to the rocket ship before it’s too late!! On your journey, you’ll dodge aliens, jump over craters, duck under meteors, and much more!!!

Welcome to Mission Possible: Dino Mission! In this fun and interactive physical education game, you will head back in time to the Late Jurassic Period where you will be tasked with helping Belinda Brontosaurus retrieve her egg from some mischievous pterodactyls. On your journey, you’ll dodge stegosauruses and triceratopses, weave through falling volcanic rock, duck under pterodactyls and much more!!!

Welcome to Mission Possible: Jungle Mission! In this fun and interactive physical education game, you will head to the jungle where you will be tasked with collecting a special gem that holds the secrets of the universe before the Evil Dr. John Jacob Jingle gets his hands on it. On your journey, you’ll dodge silverback gorillas, weave through falling coconuts, punch mangos, duck under swinging monkeys and much more!!!

Welcome to Mission Possible: Cloud Mission! In this fun and interactive physical education game, you will be tasked with rescuing Professor Skinnamarinky and retrieving the lost gem from Dr. John Jacob Jingle. On your journey, you’ll weave through lightning bolts and skydivers, punch grumpy clouds, duck under pelicans and airplanes and much more!!!

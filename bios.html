Get To Know Us A Little Better!

Shawn Matts

Shawn is an Ontario Certified Teacher with Primary/Junior/Intermediate/Senior qualifications and is currently a teacher in the Toronto District School Board. He has over 9 years of teaching experience in both Canada and South Korea with 3 years of experience working with students with various exceptionalities. Shawn graduated from Ottawa University with a Bachelor of Arts degree in Psychology and from Lakehead University with a Bachelor of Education. He has completed Additional Qualifications in Special Education, ESL, and Mathematics. Shawn is a very passionate teacher who is committed to ensuring student learning, growth, and development. When not pursuing his passion for teaching, Shawn enjoys sports, movies, and traveling.
